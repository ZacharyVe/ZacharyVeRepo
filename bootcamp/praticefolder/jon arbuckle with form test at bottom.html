About Jon Arbuckle

Hi there everyone! My name is Jon Arbuckle. I am 30 years old and I am a cartoonist that lives in Munic, Indiana. I am the proud owner of a cat named Garfield and a dog named Odie. Garfiled is fat lazy and loves to eat, while Odie is a dog without a brain.

Although my pets Drive me crazy, I cant imagine a life without them.


I spend about half my time going to the grocery store to buy enough food to feed Garfield. I have to make sure to buy all of Garfield's favorite foods, or else he'll get grumpy.
